Data Removal Request

Last updated: April 3, 2025

Your Right to Data Removal

At Uploader, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the removal of your data from our systems.

What Data We Store

We store the following types of data:

  • Account information (email, username, profile details)
  • Connected social media account tokens
  • Upload history and content metadata
  • Usage statistics and preferences
  • Communication records with our support team

How to Request Data Removal

To request the removal of your data, you can:

  1. Send an email to privacy@uploader.example.com with the subject line "Data Removal Request"
  2. Include your account email address and any specific data you want removed
  3. We will verify your identity before processing the request

What Happens After Your Request

Once we receive your request:

  • We will verify your identity within 48 hours
  • We will process your request within 30 days
  • We will send you a confirmation email once the data is removed
  • We will retain minimal information required by law for record-keeping purposes

Data Retention Requirements

Please note that we may be required to retain certain information for legal, security, or business purposes, including:

  • Records of transactions for tax purposes
  • Information required for legal compliance
  • Data necessary for fraud prevention

Third-Party Data

Please note that removing your data from our systems does not automatically remove your content from third-party platforms (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok). You will need to manage your content on those platforms separately.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the data removal process, please contact our privacy team at privacy@uploader.example.com.
